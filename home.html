How to Replace a Washer on a Tap

Step 1: Turn Off the Water Supply

Locate the water supply valve and turn it off to prevent water flow.

Step 2: Remove the Tap Handle

Use a screwdriver to remove the screw holding the tap handle in place.

Step 3: Unscrew the Valve

Use a wrench to unscrew the valve from the tap body.

Step 4: Replace the Washer

Remove the old washer and replace it with a new one of the same size.

Step 5: Reassemble the Tap

Screw the valve back into the tap body and reattach the tap handle.

Step 6: Turn On the Water Supply

Turn the water supply valve back on and check for leaks.
